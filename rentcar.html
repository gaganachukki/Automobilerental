Rental Requirements

Valid Driver's License

A valid, unexpired driver's license from your country of residence is required. International renters must also present a passport.

Minimum Age

Primary renters must be at least 25 years old. Drivers aged 21-24 may rent select vehicles subject to an additional underage surcharge.

Security Deposit

A major credit card in the renter's name is required for a security hold at the time of pickup. The deposit amount varies by vehicle tier.

Door-to-Door Delivery Service

Don't want to visit a branch? We offer complimentary white-glove delivery and pickup of your rental vehicle straight to your hotel, home, or office within a 20-mile radius of our locations.
